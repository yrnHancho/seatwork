Vincenzo

The drama centers on Italian mafia consigliere Vincenzo Cassano, who following the death of his boss relocates to Korea. He stays for a while with the intention of finding some hidden gold before leaving. When he joins the battle against Babel, a very corrupt organization of businesses, his strategy is derailed. With the aid of Korean attorney Hong Cha-Young, he ultimately ends himself defending and assisting a motley crew of residents of Geumga Plaza. In a world where justice does not exist and the law is insufficient, the drama puts evil characters against one another. With a huge ensemble, there is action, thrill, drama, and comedy.


Don Fabio, the head of the Cassano mafia family, adopted and raised Vincenzo , a Korean-Italian lawyer, in Italy.


Ok Taecyeon took on his first-ever major antagonist role in Vincenzo as Jang Han-seok, chairman of the Babel Group.


Hong Cha-young joins forces with Vincenzo and the other tenants to fight Babel Group.
